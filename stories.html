Cycling in Raratonga

My time spent in Raratonga opened my eyes and mind. The experiences ranges from learing culturally about different practices and people and the lifestyles caused me to change my outlook on what goals I would set for my life.The idea of success slowly faded away as a priority and the ideas of connection and peace slowly worked their way into my mind as priorities or simply options for priorities. I began to realize the fulfillment of my life was not limited by my abilities as much as the perspectives I held towards it.
